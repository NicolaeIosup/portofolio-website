About

My Story

I am a 26 years old Romanian boy, who decided at just 20 years old to
move away from his family and move to the United Kingdom to pursue
his dreams. I always wanted to be independent and face new challenges that will make me a better person. I started my career in Real Estate, renting and selling properties in the
seaside town of Bournemouth. I worked in this industry for 5 years and having to deal with customers at
all times, I understood how important it is to understand their problems and how to find the best solution for them in terms of properties, skills that now help me a lot in understanding the User Experience role. I always enjoyed working closely with my clients but I wanted a change in my life
and I decided to start learning about what it takes to be a UX/UI Designer. After studying a comprehensive course with CareerFoundry, I am now confident enough to say that I am looking forward to starting my career as a designer and start developing products that will solve the real problems of the users out there.
